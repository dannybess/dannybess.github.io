About us

Dali Labs was founded in 2014, by two teens, Patrick Li and Daniel Bessonov. While relatively young, Daniel and Patrick have a desire to make the world a better and more interesting place, one app at a time.

Daniel Bessonov

Daniel is a full stack developer and a teen entrepreneur. He is also the Co-Founder and President of Dali Labs, Inc.

Patrick Li

Patrick specializes in Front-End and UI/UIX development, with some experience in Back-End. He is also the Co-Founder and CEO of Dali Labs, Inc.

Reford

A new and exciting way to streamline the referee process, using cutting-edge watch connectivity technology. This can be used for local minor league soccer games all the way up to the FIFA World Cup! With this app, anyone can see the goals, penalties, and calls occuring during the match. Good news for all those referees out there! Instead of having to fumble for that piece of paper everytime anything happens, simply tap a few buttons on your apple watch, get it instantly recorded and uploaded, and have it ready for the rest of the world to check out!

Dodgy

The most fun, and most exciting emoji game in the entire world has arrived to your nearest app store. This fast paced game tests your reflexes and strategic ability to collect emoji smiley faces, and dodge the emoji poop. Try to beat your friends' highscores, and unlock new emojis as you travel through the endless space-emoji continuum.

Jumpen

Flappy bird 2.0 is here! Except with a little twist. Instead of flapping, you're jumping! The longer you hold, the higher you jump. Try to time your jump perfectly so that you can jump through the pipes without hitting the top or, bottom.
