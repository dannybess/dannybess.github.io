About us

Dali Labs was founded by Daniel Bessonov and Patrick Li in 2014. Daniel and Patrick first started Dali Labs as a learning experience, but it has since grown into a fast growing startup, filled with innovative ideas and influential products. Since it's conception, Dali Labs has grown a user base of more than 50 thousand.

Daniel Bessonov

Daniel is a mobile and web full stack developer, and the Co-Founder and President at Dali Labs, Inc. He loves coming up with new and innovative ideas, and developing them into viable products. He never passes up an opportunity to learn.

Patrick Li

Patrick is an avid entrepreneur who is the Co-Founder and CEO at Dali Labs, Inc. He is always looking for new ideas and opportunities to learn. A full stack developer, Patrick also specializes in iOS development.
